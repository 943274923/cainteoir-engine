Text Corpus

A text corpus is a collection of real-world documents and text fragments. This information can be annotated with the words as spoken and the parts of speech which can then be data mined and processed to assist the text processing phase of the text-to-speech engine. The data can also be used as a test set.

The idea is to use Public Domain or Creative Commons licensed works in the text corpus.
