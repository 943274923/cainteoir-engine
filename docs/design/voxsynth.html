Cainteoir is the Irish Gaeilge word for speaker.

Text-to-Speech

VoxSynth

The Voice Synthesiser (VoxSynth) consists of two parts: the voice engine and the synthesiser.

Voice Engine

The Voice Engine is responsible for taking the phoneme stream and associated prosody information and convert it to a series of audio generation instructions.

The engine uses voice data to assist it in this transformation.

Synthesiser

The synthesiser takes the audio generation instructions and turns them into audio. It supports different generation schemes, such as:

  1. Audio Playback — this plays a pre-recorded audio file;
  2. Klatt — this uses the Klatt formant synthesiser.
